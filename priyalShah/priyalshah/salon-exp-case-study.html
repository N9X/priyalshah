MOBILE APP DESIGN

Salon Express

My work for Salon Express was to design mobile application that is unisex and allows user to scroll through all the salons available and book an appointment.

Overview

Salon Express is a mobile application that allows user to view and book salon appointments. I was asked to designed the application from scratch. This was a super quick solo project as the client was in a eager need to publish it.

Duration

2 Weeks

Role

UX designer responsible for ideation, and visual design

Team

Solo

Tools

Adobe XD Photoshop Illustrator

Scope

The goal is to make customerâ€™s lives easier by providing them a platform to look and book an appointment saving them time and money. Due to time constraint, the scope was narrowed to UI, Visual Design, and arranging the content in a way that is navigable and discoverable.

Problem Definition

The problem was found after an initial proposal was collected. The health and beauty salon sector is increasing tremendously and is predicted to exceed $716 billion by 2025 according to an article by Shyam Arya (MD & Chairman, Indus Cosmeceuticals). Customers, however, suffer numerous issues as a result of the industry's rapid expansion, including:

  1. Customers are unable to locate or are unaware of neighboring salons.
  2. The services provided by each salon may differ and there is no way of knowing what they are.
  3. The cost of each service varies depending on the salon and the only way of knowing it, is to either call them or depend on a google review (that is if that local salon is added to the google review)
  4. The customer must wait in line for their turn, perhaps wasting time.

VISUAL DESIGN

High-Fidelity Design

01
Splash Screen

After installing the application, the splash screen pops up with the logo and takes the user to the walkthrough screen.

Users can pick whether they are a user or a business owner on the walkthrough screen.

02
Login and Register Screen

Only your name, email, pin code (zip code), and password are needed to complete the registration procedure. The process of creating a new password is straightforward, and it just necessitates the use of an email address.

03
Discover local salons

Users may view all of the area salon operators that have registered, as well as their reviews, average hourly rates, and schedules.

Users can also narrow down their choices based on the salon's reviews and pin code.

04
Business Profile Details

Users may get all of the information regarding the salon they have chosen. Info's first tab offers all of the essential information about the establishment, such as the address, hours of operation, about us, and photos. The second option, Ratings and Reviews, displays all consumer reviews and ratings. The third and last category, Services, lists all the salon's services. The top-level Book Now and Messages tabs simplify users to schedule appointments or contact the salon owner.

05
Booking Confirmation

Users can easily book an appointment from their favorite salons by selecting the services they want and getting a booking.

06
Messages

User can directly chat with the stylist and can send them a photo of they want. This allow the customer to chat their favorite stylist and ask them their availability.

07
User Profile

User can view their profile, messages, bookings, and about us. Selecting My Bookings will take them to a new screen showing them all their past and upcoming bookings along with the services they have picked.
